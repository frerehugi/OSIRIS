How it works

Set it once, invest automatically

OSIRIS handles the complexity of recurring crypto investments. You define the plan, the vault executes it on schedule — no manual swaps, no missed entries.

Step 1

⚙️

Configure your plan

Choose total amount, token allocation, and interval. Daily or weekly — you decide.

Step 2

🔒

Vault holds funds

Your USDC is deposited into a non-custodial smart contract. Only you can cancel and withdraw.

Step 3

🔄

Keeper executes

An automated keeper fetches optimal routes via Squid Router and triggers each swap on schedule.

Step 4

👛

Assets in your wallet

wBTC, wETH, CELO and XAUoT arrive directly in your wallet after every execution.

Architecture

Powered by Squid Router

OSIRIS uses Squid as its exclusive routing layer — giving access to the best available liquidity across all DEXs on Celo, including Tether Gold (XAUoT).
